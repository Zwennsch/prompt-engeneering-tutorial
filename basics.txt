ChatGPT-Prompt Engeneering - Basics

1. Clear instructions
2. Adopt a persona
    - give some specific information about who you are
3. Specify the format
    - do you want the output to be a...
        - summary
        - detailed explanation
        - checklist...
4. Avoid leading the answer
5 Limit the scope

Zero shot prompting vs Few shot prompting:

Zero shot prompting:
- prompting without any explicit training examples
- the model performs a task without having seen any examples

Few shot prompting:
- We give the model data in advance
